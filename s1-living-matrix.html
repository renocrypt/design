01 — the system what makes it agentic
Ξ

Policy, not script

A chatbot answers; an agent acts. What separates them is a policy — a way of choosing actions under uncertainty, scored by consequences, not applause.

Ω

Tools with boundaries

Capabilities arrive as instruments with edges: explicit inputs, honest outputs, and permissions you can enumerate. Power you can audit, not magic you trust.
