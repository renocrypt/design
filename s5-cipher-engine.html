Ch. 01 — anatomy

Three rotors, one secret

Each ring is a fixed substitution alphabet; each keypress steps the right rotor, carries like an odometer. 26³ starting positions, and the wiring is public — only the setting is secret.

Ch. 02 — live fire

Type — it encrypts

Your keyboard drives the machine: a key sinks, the right rotor steps, and a lamp answers with the cipher letter. Real Enigma wiring — I, II, III, reflector B.

Ch. 03 — provenance

Made, not found

No stock assets in this instrument — geometry, letter rings, keys, lamps and cables are generated at runtime. The machine is the argument.
